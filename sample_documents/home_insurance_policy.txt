HOMEOWNERS INSURANCE POLICY
Policy Number: HO-2024-456123
Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Policyholder: Michael and Lisa Brown
Property Address: 123 Oak Street, Springfield

SECTION 1: PROPERTY COVERAGE

1.1 Dwelling Coverage (Coverage A)
- Coverage Limit: $350,000
- Covers the physical structure of the home
- Includes attached structures (garage, deck, porch)
- Replacement cost coverage (no depreciation)

1.2 Other Structures (Coverage B)
- Coverage Limit: $35,000 (10% of dwelling coverage)
- Covers detached structures: shed, fence, gazebo
- Separate deductible applies

1.3 Personal Property (Coverage C)
- Coverage Limit: $175,000 (50% of dwelling coverage)
- Covers furniture, clothing, electronics, appliances
- Actual Cash Value (ACV) unless replacement cost rider purchased
- Special limits apply to jewelry ($2,500), cash ($500), firearms ($2,500)

1.4 Loss of Use (Coverage D)
- Coverage Limit: $70,000 (20% of dwelling coverage)
- Covers additional living expenses if home is uninhabitable
- Includes hotel, meals, and temporary housing
- Maximum duration: 12 months

SECTION 2: LIABILITY COVERAGE

2.1 Personal Liability (Coverage E)
- Coverage Limit: $300,000 per occurrence
- Covers bodily injury and property damage to others
- Covers legal defense costs
- Applies on and off premises

2.2 Medical Payments to Others (Coverage F)
- Coverage Limit: $5,000 per person
- Covers medical expenses for injuries on your property
- No fault coverage (pays regardless of liability)
- Does not cover household members

SECTION 3: COVERED PERILS

3.1 Named Perils Covered:
- Fire and lightning
- Windstorm and hail
- Explosion
- Riot and civil commotion
- Aircraft and vehicle damage
- Smoke damage
- Vandalism and malicious mischief
- Theft and burglary
- Falling objects
- Weight of ice, snow, or sleet
- Water damage from plumbing or appliances

3.2 Additional Coverage (with endorsement):
- Earthquake (separate deductible: 10% of dwelling coverage)
- Flood (requires separate NFIP policy)
- Sewer backup ($10,000 sublimit)

SECTION 4: EXCLUSIONS

4.1 Perils NOT Covered:
- Flood (requires separate flood insurance)
- Earthquake (unless endorsement purchased)
- War and nuclear hazards
- Intentional damage by insured
- Normal wear and tear
- Mold and fungus (limited coverage $10,000)
- Vermin, rodents, insects, or birds
- Government action (eminent domain, zoning)

4.2 Property NOT Covered:
- Business property (requires business policy)
- Vehicles and motorized equipment
- Property of renters or boarders
- Animals, birds, or fish

SECTION 5: DEDUCTIBLES

5.1 Standard Deductibles:
- All perils: $1,000 per occurrence
- Windstorm/hail: $2,500 per occurrence (coastal areas)
- Earthquake (if purchased): 10% of dwelling coverage
- Water damage: $2,500 per occurrence

5.2 Deductible Waiver:
- Fire department service charge: No deductible
- Credit card fraud: No deductible
- Debris removal: Included in coverage, no separate deductible

SECTION 6: PREMIUM AND PAYMENT

6.1 Annual Premium: $1,450
6.2 Payment Options:
- Annual: $1,450 (full payment discount applied)
- Semi-annual: $750 per payment ($1,500 total)
- Quarterly: $380 per payment ($1,520 total)
- Monthly: $130 per payment ($1,560 total)

6.3 Discounts Applied:
- Multi-policy discount: 15% (bundled with auto insurance)
- Security system: 10%
- Smoke detectors: 5%
- Claims-free discount: 10%

SECTION 7: POLICY CONDITIONS

7.1 Duties After Loss:
- Notify insurer immediately (within 24 hours for theft)
- Protect property from further damage
- Prepare inventory of damaged property
- Provide receipts and documentation
- Cooperate with investigation

7.2 Appraisal and Dispute Resolution:
- Either party may demand appraisal
- Each party selects an appraiser
- Appraisers select an umpire
- Decision is binding

SECTION 8: SPECIAL LIMITS OF LIABILITY

8.1 The following limits apply to these categories:
- Jewelry, watches, furs: $2,500 total
- Cash, gold, silver: $500 total
- Securities, stamps, coins: $1,500 total
- Watercraft and trailers: $1,500 total
- Firearms: $2,500 total
- Business property: $2,500 total
- Electronic equipment in vehicle: $1,500 total

8.2 Increased Limits Available:
- Scheduled personal property endorsement available
- Floater policies for high-value items
- Requires appraisal for items over $5,000

SECTION 9: ADDITIONAL COVERAGES

9.1 Included at No Extra Charge:
- Debris removal: Up to 25% of dwelling coverage
- Reasonable repairs: Actual cost to protect property
- Trees, shrubs, plants: $500 per item, $5,000 total
- Fire department charges: $500
- Property removal: 30 days coverage at other location
- Credit card and fund transfer fraud: $500
- Lock replacement after theft: Actual cost

SECTION 10: CLAIMS PROCESS

10.1 Filing a Claim:
- Contact claims department: 1-800-CLAIMS-1
- Online filing available at www.insurance.com/claims
- Adjuster assigned within 24-48 hours
- Initial inspection within 3-5 business days

10.2 Claim Settlement:
- Actual Cash Value paid initially for personal property
- Replacement cost paid after repairs completed or items replaced
- Settlement within 30 days of agreement
- Dwelling repairs: Replacement cost without depreciation

SECTION 11: CANCELLATION AND NON-RENEWAL

11.1 Cancellation by Insured:
- May cancel at any time with written notice
- Pro-rated refund for unused premium
- Minimum earned premium: $50

11.2 Cancellation by Insurer:
- 30 days notice required (except 10 days for non-payment)
- Valid reasons: material misrepresentation, increased hazard, fraud
- Pro-rated refund provided

11.3 Non-Renewal:
- 60 days notice required before expiration
- No reason required for non-renewal
- Insured has right to appeal
