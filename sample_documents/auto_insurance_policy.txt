AUTO INSURANCE POLICY
Policy Number: AI-2024-789456
Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Policyholder: John Smith

SECTION 1: COVERAGE SUMMARY

1.1 Liability Coverage
- Bodily Injury Liability: $250,000 per person / $500,000 per accident
- Property Damage Liability: $100,000 per accident
- Coverage applies to accidents where the insured is at fault

1.2 Collision Coverage
- Deductible: $500
- Covers damage to insured vehicle from collision with another vehicle or object
- Maximum payout: Actual Cash Value (ACV) of vehicle

1.3 Comprehensive Coverage
- Deductible: $250
- Covers theft, vandalism, fire, natural disasters, and animal collisions
- Does NOT cover mechanical breakdown or wear and tear

1.4 Uninsured/Underinsured Motorist Coverage
- Bodily Injury: $250,000 per person / $500,000 per accident
- Property Damage: $50,000 per accident

SECTION 2: EXCLUSIONS

2.1 The following are NOT covered under this policy:
- Intentional damage caused by the insured
- Commercial use of vehicle (ridesharing, delivery services)
- Racing or competitive driving events
- Driving under the influence of alcohol or drugs
- Using vehicle for illegal activities
- Damage from nuclear hazards or war

2.2 Pre-Existing Damage
- Any damage that existed before policy effective date is not covered
- Vehicle must pass initial inspection

SECTION 3: DEDUCTIBLES AND PREMIUMS

3.1 Annual Premium: $1,850
3.2 Payment Options: Monthly ($160), Quarterly ($470), Annual ($1,850 with 5% discount)

3.3 Deductible Structure:
- Collision: $500 per incident
- Comprehensive: $250 per incident
- Glass damage: No deductible for windshield repair

SECTION 4: CLAIMS PROCESS

4.1 Reporting Requirements
- Report accident within 24 hours of incident
- Provide police report for accidents involving other parties
- Submit photos and documentation within 48 hours

4.2 Claims Timeline
- Initial response: Within 24 hours
- Damage assessment: Within 3-5 business days
- Payment processing: Within 10-15 business days after approval

SECTION 5: POLICY MODIFICATIONS

5.1 Coverage can be increased or decreased with 30 days notice
5.2 Adding/removing drivers requires immediate notification
5.3 Vehicle replacement must be reported within 30 days

SECTION 6: CANCELLATION POLICY

6.1 Insured may cancel at any time with written notice
6.2 Insurer may cancel for non-payment after 15-day grace period
6.3 Pro-rated refund provided for early cancellation by insured
