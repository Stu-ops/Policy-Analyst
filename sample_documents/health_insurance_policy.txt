COMPREHENSIVE HEALTH INSURANCE POLICY
Policy Number: HI-2024-123789
Effective Date: January 1, 2024
Policyholder: Sarah Johnson

SECTION 1: COVERAGE OVERVIEW

1.1 Medical Benefits
- Annual Maximum Coverage: $2,000,000
- Lifetime Maximum: $5,000,000
- In-Network Deductible: $1,500 individual / $3,000 family
- Out-of-Network Deductible: $3,000 individual / $6,000 family

1.2 Coinsurance
- In-Network: 80/20 (Plan pays 80%, member pays 20%)
- Out-of-Network: 60/40 (Plan pays 60%, member pays 40%)
- Out-of-Pocket Maximum: $6,000 individual / $12,000 family

SECTION 2: COVERED SERVICES

2.1 Preventive Care (100% covered, no deductible)
- Annual physical examination
- Routine immunizations
- Cancer screenings (mammogram, colonoscopy)
- Well-child visits
- Annual wellness visit

2.2 Inpatient Hospital Care
- Room and board (semi-private room)
- Surgery and anesthesia
- Emergency room visits ($250 copay, waived if admitted)
- Intensive care unit (ICU)
- Physician services during hospitalization

2.3 Outpatient Services
- Office visits: $30 copay (primary care), $50 copay (specialist)
- Urgent care: $75 copay
- Diagnostic tests and X-rays: Subject to deductible and coinsurance
- Physical therapy: $40 copay, maximum 30 visits per year

2.4 Prescription Drug Coverage
- Generic drugs: $10 copay
- Preferred brand: $35 copay
- Non-preferred brand: $70 copay
- Specialty medications: 30% coinsurance, maximum $200 per prescription

2.5 Mental Health Services
- Inpatient psychiatric care: Same as medical/surgical
- Outpatient therapy: $30 copay per visit
- Substance abuse treatment: Covered at same level as mental health

SECTION 3: EXCLUSIONS AND LIMITATIONS

3.1 Services NOT Covered:
- Cosmetic surgery (unless medically necessary)
- Experimental or investigational treatments
- Infertility treatments (IVF, fertility drugs)
- Weight loss programs and surgery
- Hearing aids (except for children under 18)
- Routine dental and vision care (separate policies required)

3.2 Pre-Existing Conditions
- All pre-existing conditions ARE covered
- No waiting period for pre-existing condition coverage
- Coverage begins on policy effective date

3.3 Limitations:
- Chiropractic care: Maximum 20 visits per year
- Acupuncture: Maximum 12 visits per year
- Home health care: Maximum 100 visits per year
- Skilled nursing facility: Maximum 60 days per year

SECTION 4: PREMIUM INFORMATION

4.1 Monthly Premium Rates:
- Individual: $450
- Individual + Spouse: $850
- Individual + Children: $750
- Family: $1,200

4.2 Premium Payment:
- Due on the 1st of each month
- 15-day grace period for payment
- Coverage terminates if payment not received within grace period

SECTION 5: CLAIMS AND REIMBURSEMENT

5.1 In-Network Claims
- Provider submits claims directly
- Member receives Explanation of Benefits (EOB)
- Member pays deductible, copay, and coinsurance directly to provider

5.2 Out-of-Network Claims
- Member may need to pay in full and submit claim for reimbursement
- Submit itemized bills within 90 days of service
- Reimbursement processed within 30 days of claim receipt

SECTION 6: EMERGENCY SERVICES

6.1 Emergency Room Coverage
- $250 copay (waived if admitted)
- No prior authorization required
- Covered at in-network level even if out-of-network facility

6.2 Ambulance Services
- Emergency ambulance: Covered at 100% after deductible
- Non-emergency ambulance: Requires prior authorization

SECTION 7: SPECIAL PROVISIONS

7.1 Maternity and Newborn Care
- Prenatal care: Covered as preventive (100%)
- Delivery and postpartum: Subject to deductible and coinsurance
- Newborn care: Automatically covered for 31 days, must add to policy after

7.2 Preventive Care for Chronic Conditions
- Diabetes supplies and education: Covered
- Asthma management: Covered
- Heart disease prevention: Covered

SECTION 8: APPEAL PROCESS

8.1 If claim is denied:
- Internal appeal within 180 days of denial
- External review available if internal appeal denied
- Expedited appeals for urgent situations (72-hour response)
